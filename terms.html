1.0 Authorization

Access is strictly limited to verified U.S. residents. Users must undergo Identity Authentication (KYC) as per Patriot Act requirements to qualify for disbursement.

2.0 Federal Fraud Statutes

Intentional misrepresentation to obtain federal funds is a felony. Under 18 U.S.C. § 1001, false statements are punishable by up to 5 years in prison and $250,000 in fines.

3.0 Disbursement Protocols

Funds are released via the Federal Reserve Bank (FedWire). Transfers are only permitted to accounts matching the verified applicant's legal identity.

4.0 Privacy & Encryption

PII and banking data are secured using 256-bit AES encryption. Data usage is limited to grant allocation and disbursement verification under the Privacy Act of 1974.
